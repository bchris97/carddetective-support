# Card Detective Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

## Overview
Card Detective is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

## Information We Collect

**Device ID**
We generate an anonymous device identifier to track your monthly scan usage. This ID is not linked to your name, email, or Apple ID.

**Card Images**
Photos you take to scan cards are sent to our server for identification and are not stored after processing.

**Collection Data**
Cards you save to your collection are stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to your collection.

**Purchase Information**
Subscription purchases are handled by Apple. We receive confirmation of your subscription status through RevenueCat but do not receive your payment details.

## How We Use Information
- Device ID is used solely to enforce the monthly free scan limit
- Card images are used solely to identify the card and retrieve pricing
- We do not sell, share, or monetize any user data

## Third Party Services
Card Detective uses the following third party services:
- **Anthropic Claude** — for AI card identification
- **eBay Browse API** — for live market pricing
- **RevenueCat** — for subscription management

## Data Retention
Card images are not retained after processing. Device IDs are stored in memory only and reset monthly.
