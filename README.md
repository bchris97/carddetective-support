## Frequently Asked Questions

**How many free scans do I get?**
Free users get 10 scans per month. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited scans.

**How does card identification work?**
Card Detective uses AI to analyze your card photo and identify the player, year, brand, set, and condition.

**Where does pricing data come from?**
Pricing is pulled from live eBay listings to reflect current market values. Prices shown are averages based on recent listings and may vary from individual sales.

**Why does my card show a different price than I see on eBay?**
Prices fluctuate constantly. Card Detective filters out graded cards, lot listings, and outliers to give you the most accurate price for an ungraded single card.

**My card was misidentified. What should I do?**
Try scanning again in better lighting with the full card visible. For best results, lay the card flat on a plain surface.

**How do I restore my Pro subscription?**
Open Card Detective, tap the Pro button, and tap "Restore Purchases".

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Card Detective.
